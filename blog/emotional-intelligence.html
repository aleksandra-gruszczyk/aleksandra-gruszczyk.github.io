Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence is defined as the ability to be aware of, understand and manage emotions in oneself and others. To me, emotional intelligence is inextricably linked with empathy. One of the main elements of EI is the ability to put oneself in other people's shoes: to be able to - and to be willing to - understand the emotional states of others. The other key aspect of EI is the self-awareness: the comprehension of one's own emotional states, of how the emotions affect our actions and our perception of the world. Emotional Intelligence can be trained, and I'd say that in our deeply social world it moreover should be trained.
